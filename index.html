Get Answers to All Your Questions.
Individuals

What halls do you have?

Top Event offers both banquet rooms and halls for lectures, conferences, concerts, etc.

What is the maximum capacity of your largest hall?

Our largest hall is Majestic, it can accommodate up to 150 people.

What if I don’t know which hall to choose?

Our managers will offer you the best options for your needs. We will also help you organize the event and introduce you to reliable contractors.

How long does it take to organize an event?

It all depends on the type of your event and requirements. It is better to contact us in advance so that the halls and equipment are not booked.

Companies

Can our decorator work at your venue?

Yes, after signing the contract, your employees can start preparing our venue for the event.

What equipment can we rent?

Top Event provides any sound and multimedia equipment for holding events - projectors, computer equipment, audio systems, and much more.

Who should I contact if I have a special request?

Each event is accompanied by a personal manager. (S)He works with the client at all stages. If you have any special requests, just tell her or him.

What is the payment scheme?

When organizing events at Top Event, our client should make a deposit for the rental of the hall and equipment. The amount is discussed individually. The calculation of the full cost is made by your personal manager.
